# Fuel Finder — Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 12 February 2026

## Overview

Fuel Finder is a fuel price comparison app for UK drivers. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the app accesses and how it is used.

## Data We Collect

**We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

## Location Data

Fuel Finder requests access to your device's location to:

- Find fuel stations near your current position
- Calculate routes between your origin and destination
- Show your location on the map

Your location is processed **entirely on your device**. It is sent only to Apple's MapKit services for route calculation and map display. We do not receive, log, or store your location data on any server.

## Fuel Price Data

The app retrieves fuel price data from the UK Government's open data feed. These requests do not include any personally identifiable information.

## iCloud Sync

If enabled, Fuel Finder uses Apple's CloudKit to sync your favourites and settings across your devices. This data is stored in your private iCloud account and is not accessible to us.

## Third-Party Services

Fuel Finder does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

| Service | Purpose | Data shared |
|---------|---------|-------------|
| Apple MapKit | Route calculation and map display | Coordinates (on-device) |
| UK Gov Fuel Data | Fuel station prices | None (public API) |
| Apple CloudKit | Favourites sync | Your private iCloud only |

## Data Retention

We retain no user data. All app data is stored locally on your device or in your private iCloud account.

## Children's Privacy

Fuel Finder does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

Any updates to this policy will be reflected in the app's GitHub repository with an updated date.
