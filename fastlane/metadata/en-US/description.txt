Find the cheapest fuel near you or along your route with Fuel Finder — the simple, fast way to save on every journey.

Fuel Finder pulls live prices directly from UK retailer open data feeds to help you compare petrol and diesel prices. Browse nearby stations or plan a route to see fuel stops along the way.

KEY FEATURES

Map & List Views
See nearby fuel stations on an interactive map or browse as a sorted list. Switch between price and distance sorting to find the best value or closest stop.

Route Planning
Enter a destination and Fuel Finder shows you fuel stations along your route. Adjust the search radius from 1 to 5 miles to see more or fewer options. Each station shows detour distance so you can make informed decisions.

Live Price Comparison
Prices are sourced directly from retailer open data feeds (Asda, BP, Esso, JET, Morrisons, Moto, Motor Fuel Group, Rontec, Sainsbury's, SGN, Shell, Tesco) published under the CMA fuel price transparency scheme. Data is refreshed every 12 hours.

Smart Scoring
Every station is scored using a blend of price and distance. Green badges highlight the cheapest options; amber and red show mid-range and expensive stations at a glance.

Directions via Apple Maps
Tap any station to view full details, then tap Get Directions to hand off to Apple Maps for turn-by-turn navigation.

CarPlay Integration
Use Fuel Finder on your car's display with native CarPlay support. Browse stations on a map or list, view prices, change fuel type and sort order, and hand off navigation to Apple Maps — all without touching your phone.

Favourites & iCloud Sync
Star your preferred stations and access them across all your devices via iCloud.

Multiple Fuel Types
Compare prices for unleaded E10, super unleaded E5, diesel B7, and premium diesel SDV. Switch fuel types instantly to see updated pricing.

Designed for the UK
Built specifically for UK drivers using official retailer open data. No API keys required, no subscriptions, completely free to use.

PRIVACY
Fuel Finder uses your location only to find nearby stations and calculate routes. No tracking, no ads, no data sold to third parties. Your location data never leaves your device except to calculate routes via Apple's MapKit framework.
