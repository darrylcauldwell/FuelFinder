Find the cheapest fuel near you with Fuel Finder — the simple, fast way to save on every fill-up.

Fuel Finder pulls live prices directly from UK retailer open data feeds to help you compare petrol and diesel at stations near your current location. Pick the best value stop and get directions with a single tap.

KEY FEATURES

Nearby Station Search
See the cheapest fuel stations within 16 km of you — ranked by price and distance so you can choose the best stop without wasting time or money.

Live Price Comparison
Prices are sourced directly from retailer open data feeds (Asda, BP, Esso, JET, Morrisons, Moto, Motor Fuel Group, Rontec, Sainsbury's, SGN, Shell, Tesco) published under the CMA fuel price transparency scheme. Data is refreshed every 12 hours.

Smart Scoring
Every station is scored using a blend of price and distance. Green badges highlight the cheapest options; amber and red show mid-range and expensive stations at a glance.

Directions via Apple Maps
Tap any station to view full details, then tap Get Directions to hand off to Apple Maps for turn-by-turn navigation.

CarPlay Support
Use Fuel Finder on your car's display. Browse nearby stations and favourites, view prices, and navigate — all without touching your phone.

Favourites & CloudKit Sync
Star your preferred stations and access them across all your devices via iCloud.

Designed for the UK
Covers petrol (unleaded E10 & super unleaded E5) and diesel (standard B7 & premium SDV).

PRIVACY
Fuel Finder uses your location only to find nearby stations. No tracking, no ads, no data sold to third parties.
