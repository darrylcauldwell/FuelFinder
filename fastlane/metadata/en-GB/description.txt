Find the cheapest fuel near you or along your route with Fuel Finder — the smart way to save on every journey.

Fuel Finder shows you live petrol and diesel prices at thousands of UK forecourts. Browse nearby stations, plan routes to see fuel stops along the way, and compare prices with a single tap.

KEY FEATURES

Map & List Views
See nearby fuel stations on an interactive map or browse as a sorted list. Switch between price and distance sorting to find the best value or closest stop.

Route Planning
Enter a destination and Fuel Finder shows you fuel stations along your route. Adjust the search radius from 1 to 5 miles to see more or fewer options. Each station shows the detour distance so you can make informed decisions.

Live Price Data
Powered by UK retailer open data feeds published under the CMA fuel price transparency scheme. Prices are updated regularly across 12 major retailers including Shell, BP, Tesco, Sainsbury's, Morrisons, Asda, and more.

Smart Scoring
Every station is scored using a blend of price and distance. Green badges highlight the cheapest options; amber and red show mid-range and expensive stations at a glance.

CarPlay Integration
Use Fuel Finder on your car's display with native CarPlay support. Browse stations on a map or list, view prices, change fuel type and sort order, and hand off navigation to Apple Maps — all without touching your phone.

Favourites & iCloud Sync
Star your preferred stations and access them across all your devices. Favourites sync automatically via iCloud.

Multiple Fuel Types
Compare prices for unleaded, super unleaded, diesel, and premium diesel. Switch fuel types instantly to see updated pricing.

Designed for the UK
Built specifically for UK drivers using official retailer open data. No API keys required, no subscriptions, completely free to use.

PRIVACY
Fuel Finder uses your location only to find nearby stations and calculate routes. No tracking, no ads, no data sold to third parties. Your location data never leaves your device except to calculate routes via Apple's MapKit framework.
